Summary

Dhurandhar is an upcoming Hindi action–espionage thriller. The film is expected to revolve around covert intelligence operations, deception, and power struggles, exploring the grey areas of patriotism and morality. True to its title (which means cunning or strategist), the story focuses on sharp minds, hidden agendas, and high-stakes missions rather than straightforward heroics.

Actors

  • Ranveer Singh – plays the undercover RAW agent Hamza Ali Mazari (lead protagonist)
  • Akshaye Khanna – portrays gangster/antagonist Rehman Dakait
  • Sanjay Dutt – appears as SP Chaudhary Aslam (police officer)
  • R. Madhavan – plays the Indian spymaster Ajay Sanyal
  • Arjun Rampal – features as ISI Major Iqbal (antagonistic role)
  • Sara Arjun – plays the female lead opposite Ranveer Singh
  • Danish Pandor – Uzair Baloch (Right-hand gangster)
